Problem

Smallholder farmers face climate volatility, water scarcity, and plant disease—leading to low yields and margins.

Unpredictable Weather
Outdoor crops are exposed to heat waves, floods, and erratic seasons.
Resource Inefficiency
Water and inputs are over‑applied due to missing, real‑time data.
Knowledge Gap
Farmers lack predictive insights and timely guidance for action.

Solution

A full‑stack AI + IoT Smart Greenhouse that senses, predicts, and automates.

Sense
Edge sensors stream temperature, humidity, soil moisture, EC, and light into Azure IoT Hub.
Predict
Azure ML models flag disease risk, optimize fertigation, and forecast harvest windows.
Act
Automation controls irrigation, fans, vents; farmers get WhatsApp/SMS nudges.
