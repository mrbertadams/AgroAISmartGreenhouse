Problem

  • Climate volatility causes crop failure and income instability.
  • Water & input waste due to lack of real‑time data and control.
  • Disease & pests detected late; actions are reactive, not predictive.
  • Knowledge gap for smallholders on timing, dosage, and best practices.

Solution — Full‑stack AI Smart Greenhouse

Sense: Edge sensors capture temp, humidity, soil moisture/EC, light → Azure IoT Hub.
Predict: Azure ML models optimize fertigation, flag disease risk, forecast harvest windows.
Act: Automated irrigation/ventilation; WhatsApp/SMS nudges for growers.
